I am a third year PhD Candidate in Statistical Science at Duke University working with Sayan Mukherjee, Mike West and Peter Hoff. My research interests are broadly in time series, machine learning and Bayesian inference. Current research projects focus on developing time series models that perform probabilistic inference and forecasting, accurately represent uncertainty and achieve high forecast accuracy.
